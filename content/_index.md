## Discover your Dutch level — quickly and online

A short online intake that gives you:
- a clear indication of your Dutch level (CEFR)
- personal course advice
- insight into whether an intensive course suits you

---

## Why an online intake?

Learning Dutch in an intensive course requires:
- a solid starting level
- the ability to learn fast
- time‑efficient study habits

Our intake helps us give **honest advice** before you start.

---

## What does the intake test?

### Listening
Can you understand everyday conversations?

### Reading
Can you understand short texts and messages?

### Speaking & writing
Can you express yourself in basic Dutch?

### Learning ability
How quickly do you pick up new language patterns?

---

## Practical information

- Duration: about 60 minutes  
- Fully online  
- You need sound and a microphone  
- You receive your result by e‑mail
